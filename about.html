About Us

We, the members of the above mentioned Non-Governmental/Non-Profit Making/Charity Organisation wish to give you a brief update of our achievements. Founded in 2008, with the passion to help the less priviledged and reduce maternal and perinatal mortality. Dedicated to my fellow human beings especially mother, baby and adolescent who had, are, is and will suffer/suffered/sufferring physically, mentally, socially, economically, educationally or died because of the scourge of disease, illeteracy, poverty and/or ignorance. Dr. Eli Sukarime.
At Mother, Baby and Adolescent Care Global Foundation, we have been providing affordable primary healthcare to low income and unpreviledged communitites in Rivers State.

Mother, Baby And Adolescent Care Global Foundation is an organization striving to address the gap in the availabilty of low cost primary for rural and urban under previlidged communites.

About The Founder

Dr. Eli Sukarime

Dr. Eli Sukarime is a Consultant, Obstetrician and Gynaecologist based in Port Harcourt, Nigeria. He graduated from the University of Benin, Nigeria with the degree in medicine and surgery. He is a volunterer lecturer to the Residents in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Rivers State University Teaching Hospital. He is the president and editor-in-chief of Greener Journal of Medical Sciences International, Assistant editor of Pacific International Journal and reviewer Of Nigeria Journal of Medicine. He has over 80 publications - local and international journals. He is the Executive Director/ CEO of Mother, Baby and Adolescent Care Global Foundation. Also, consultant Gynaecology, Military Hospital and First Rivers Hospital, Rivers State, Nigeria.
